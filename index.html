About us

OBA Leadership Academy is a boys’ boarding school located in Ngatatoek, Kajiado County, Kenya. It serves learners from Grade 4 to Form 3, offering a supportive and disciplined environment.

Built on strong Islamic values with a dedicated Tahfidhul Qur’an section, students memorize the Qur’an while pursuing academic excellence.

The campus supports a wide range of activities and features modern facilities such as dormitories, classrooms, and recreational areas.

“Graduating Boys to Leaders”

ACADEMICS

Upper Primary (Grade 4–6)

Our Upper Primary section provides a strong academic foundation, focusing on key competencies in numeracy, literacy, science, and Islamic studies. The curriculum is aligned with the CBC framework, ensuring students acquire critical thinking and problem-solving skills from an early age. With committed teachers, well-equipped classrooms, and individualized attention, our pupils consistently perform well in national assessments and progress confidently into junior secondary school.

Junior Secondary (Grade 7–9)

Our Junior Secondary program is designed to help students discover their strengths, develop independence, and build character. Learners engage in both core and elective subjects while continuing to receive spiritual guidance through daily Qur’an recitation and Islamic teachings. We offer mentorship programs, ICT training, and co-curricular activities that nurture both leadership and academic excellence.

Senior Secondary (Form 2–3)

In our Senior Section, students prepare for national examinations while also being mentored to become responsible, faith-driven young men. The environment is structured yet supportive, enabling students to focus on academic success, personal development, and career goals. Our teachers use learner-centered methods and regular assessments to ensure every boy is on track to graduate as a confident leader.

Tahfidhul Qur’an Section

The Tahfidhul Qur’an section is the heart of our spiritual training. We offer a dedicated and disciplined program for Qur’an memorization, led by qualified Ustaadhs who provide personalized coaching based on each student's pace and ability. Students in this section also access general education, balancing both Islamic and academic knowledge. We host regular Qur’an competitions and engage students in community da’wah activities to boost their confidence and spiritual responsibility
